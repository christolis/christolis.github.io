About me

Greetings, person or robot who is reading this! I’m Chris, a freelancer and entrepreneur from Athens, Greece. I am currently studying Computer Science and Telecommunications at the University of Thessaly. Since the age of 9, I have always been greatly fascinated by how things worked, so I've always made it a priority to learn about them. These things ranged from computer programs, operating systems or hardware, and even business and economics.

Now, as a university student who is still undone exploring this vast and enormous world, I develop useful applications and projects for people to use. Some of my main programming languages I am fluent in are Java, C/C++ and Python. It doesn't end there however as it is one of my top priorities to constantly improve myself and stay updated with newer technologies.

When you see me taking a break off of my career, you'll probably find me watching videos on YouTube, reading books, listening to music, working out, biking long distances, or travelling to places. My ultimate goals would be to study artificial intelligence abroad, become a leader of a succesful tech business and spend my life travelling the world while being free from life's daily stress.
